The Sibling Connection

What happens when a passion for the grill meets a love for the perfect scoop? You get kayy. Kafe & The Scoop.

Founded by a brother and sister duo, our space was built on the belief that a great meal isn't finished until the dessert arrives. He brings the fire with signature grills and savory bites at kayy. Kafe, while she masters the art of artisan coffee and hand-scooped treats at The Scoop.

A unique sibling-run destination where savory grills meet artisan sweets. Two concepts, one unforgettable experience.
